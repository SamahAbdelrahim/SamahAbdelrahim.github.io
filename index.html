About Me

Hello! I'm Samah (or in Arabic سماح), A PhD student in the psychology department at Stanford University. I work with Michael C. Frank

I identify as a developmental psychologist under construction :D

I love thinking about how children do all that they do; but specifically how they acquire language

When I am not in building 420, neither thinking about children, I like listening to Arabic music, watching TV, chatting with Family and friends, and reading about Islamic Sufism

Background

I was born and raised in Khartoum, Sudan; just where the River Nile comes to existence. Before my time at Stanford, I did a bachelor in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Khartoum.
